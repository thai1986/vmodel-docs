Safety and Standards
--------------------

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 1
   :widths: 20 80

   * - Abbreviation
     - Meaning
   * - ASIL-A
     - Automotive Safety Integrity Level A (lowest functional safety)
   * - ASIL-B
     - Automotive Safety Integrity Level B
   * - ASIL-C
     - Automotive Safety Integrity Level C
   * - ASIL-D
     - Automotive Safety Integrity Level D (highest functional safety)
   * - CISPR
     - Comité International Spécial des Perturbations Radioélectriques
   * - FIT
     - Failures In Time (failures per billion hours)
   * - ISO 11898
     - CAN specification standard
   * - ISO 14229
     - Unified Diagnostic Services (UDS) standard
   * - ISO 15765
     - Diagnostic communication over CAN (ISO-TP)
   * - ISO 17356
     - FlexRay communications system standard
   * - ISO 26262
     - Functional safety for road vehicles
   * - ISO 7637
     - Electrical disturbances by conduction and coupling
   * - ISO/SAE 21434
     - Road vehicles - Cybersecurity engineering
   * - QM
     - Quality Management (no ASIL classification)
