Document Review and Approval
-----------------------------

This documentation undergoes regular review:

* **Technical Review**: Software architects and lead engineers
* **Safety Review**: Functional safety manager
* **Quality Review**: Quality assurance team
* **Management Approval**: Project manager

Review Cycle
^^^^^^^^^^^^

* **Minor Updates**: As needed, reviewed within 1 week
* **Major Updates**: Quarterly, comprehensive review
* **Release Reviews**: Before each release, full validation
