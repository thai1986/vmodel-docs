Requirements Coverage Analysis
================================

This page shows the coverage of requirements by test specifications.

Overview
--------

Requirements traceability ensures that:

* Every requirement is verified by at least one test
* Every test is linked to at least one requirement
* Bidirectional traceability is maintained
* Coverage gaps are identified
